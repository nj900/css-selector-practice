Lasagna

Ingredient & Instructions

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic powder and garlic salt
  • 1/2 dry lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 lb Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 lb Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar

Step 1: Put pasta water on to boil
Step 2: Brown the ground beef
Step 3: Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
Step 4: Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
Step 5: Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
Step 6: Assemble the lasagna
Step 7: Bake
